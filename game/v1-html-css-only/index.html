How To Play

To win the Fish Frenzy, be the first penguin to snag 30 fish! On your turn, roll the dice to add fish to your bucket, but watch out for the slippery ice: if you roll a single "1", your fish slip away and your turn ends immediately. You can keep rolling to stack your catch as high as you dare, or choose to pass the turn, safely storing the fish in your bucket. Be careful not to get too greedy, though—rolling Snake Eyes (double 1s) causes a total wipeout, melting your entire game score back to zero.
